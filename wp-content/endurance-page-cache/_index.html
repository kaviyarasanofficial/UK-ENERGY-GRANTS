What Home Improvements are available?

Internal wall insulation

Internal wall insulation is done by fitting insulation backed plasterboards to the walls. The system is then sealed and a skim type finish is applied. This will help reduce the amount of heat lost through the insulated walls which can help you use less energy.

Airsource heat pump

An air-source heat pump can provide efficient heating and cooling for your home.

Solar

Solar panels take advantage of one of natures most powerful yet free resources: the energy produced by the sun.

Loft insulation

Loft insulation is a popular and cost effective measure that is used in many homes. We can also provide a competitive quotes if you don't qualify for a grant.

First time central heating

First-time central heating installations are now available under the Energy Company Obligation (ECO) scheme. You must have a mains gas connection and no history of central heating at the property.
